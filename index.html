Welcome to Carcom Lake View.

Come to Moshav Carcom to enjoy a relaxing and pampering vacation in front of the Sea of Galilee! Equipped and air-conditioned vacation apartments with a pampering jacuzzi, a well-kept yard with a pool facing the spectacular view of the Sea of Galilee and the Golan Heights.

The settlement of Carcom is located north of the Sea of Galilee and above the Jordan Park, a few minutes' drive from the shores of the Sea of Galilee, the Golan Heights and the mountainous Jordan. You can reach Karchum from the Amiad junction on road no. 90 in the direction of Tiberias until the Red HaGalil junction, from there turn east and continue for about 5 km until the entrance to the settlement.
